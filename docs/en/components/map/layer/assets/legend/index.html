Buildup Index — legend

The Buildup Index (BUI) is a component of the Canadian Forest Fire Weather Index (FWI) System. It is an indicator of the total amount of fuel available for combustion. Higher values indicate drier fuels overall and more fuel consumption during a fire. More consumption on a fire creates higher intensities and leads to more challenges to control it.

Value ranges

The legend classifies BUI values into five ranges, from lowest (least dry fuel available) to highest (driest, most fuel available):

  1. 0 to 20
  2. 21 to 40
  3. 41 to 60
  4. 61 to 90
  5. 91 or greater
